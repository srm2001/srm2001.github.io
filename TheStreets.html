Chapter 2 - The Streets

See Character Details

The streets are chaotic as you step out into them. The crowds bustling along, the cars horns honking and the sound of an aeroplane overhead.

You step out onto the streets and start to walk. The address you'd found inside the hotel is clear in your mind and after a few minutes lost you finally manage to find a series of street signs to help you orient yourself.

To the Bleeding Rose Casino! To Giovanni & Sons Morgue.

You were utterly starving and being around all these people was making it much worse. Stumbling through the crowd with all their hearbeat pounding in your ears it takes all your self control not to throw yourself at the people around you. Eventually you can't take it anymore and stumble into an alley to get away from it all...
